Who We Are

About SAYA

A youth-driven movement transforming agriculture in Zambia and beyond

Our Vision

To create a generation of empowered young agricultural leaders driving sustainable development, food security, and technological innovation across Zambia and beyond.

Our Mission

To empower youth and farming communities through knowledge, sustainable agriculture, technology adoption, market access, entrepreneurship, and inclusive collaboration across all agricultural value chains.

Core Values

Sustainability

Promoting environmental stewardship and long-term agricultural viability

Innovation

Embracing modern practices and technology-driven solutions

Integrity

Ensuring transparency and accountability in all operations

Collaboration

Working with farmers, government, private sector, and institutions

Youth Empowerment

Building skills, leadership, and opportunities for young people

Food Safety

Promoting safe, toxin-free, high-quality agricultural products

Our Goals

  • Empower 10,000+ youth in sustainable agriculture by 2025
  • Establish 50+ youth-led farming cooperatives across Zambia
  • Implement technology-driven solutions in 100+ farming communities
  • Partner with 20+ organizations for collaborative impact
  • Promote food security and environmental sustainability

Our Initiatives

Projects

Active programs driving sustainable agricultural development

Youth Agricultural Leadership Program (YALP)

A comprehensive training initiative equipping young people with skills in sustainable farming, business development, and leadership. Includes hands-on training in 20 communities across Zambia.

Partner: Ministry of Agriculture

Digital Farmer Platform

Mobile and web-based platform providing farmers with real-time market prices, weather information, and agricultural advice. Developed in partnership with Hapitech.

Partner: Hapitech

Sustainable Maize Production Initiative

Promoting climate-resilient maize varieties and conservation agriculture practices in drought-prone areas. Targets 100 farming households with improved seed varieties and training.

Partner: FAO Zambia

Strategic Partnership with Hapitech

SAYA proudly collaborates with Hapitech, a leading technology company in Zambia, to integrate innovative solutions into our agricultural programs. Together, we're building a future where technology and tradition work hand-in-hand for sustainable development.
